## Grok Image Generation Prompt

### Primary Prompt (Copy & Paste Ready)

```
Create a traditional architectural watercolor illustration of the Benjamin Siegel House, a grand Italian Renaissance Revival limestone mansion designed by architect Albert Kahn in 1915.

The building should be rendered as a stately three-story mansion constructed entirely of warm cream and buff-colored limestone. Show elegant arched windows arranged with classical symmetry, a prominent entry portico supported by classical columns with a triangular pediment, decorative wrought iron balconies, and a hipped roof covered in warm terracotta clay tiles with multiple chimneys rising above.

Use the following watercolor technique: controlled yet loose brushwork with visible paint strokes. Apply a warm, limited earth-tone palette - russet brown and gray-brown tones for shadows on the limestone, honey oak and cedar brown for wood trim elements, and soft sage greens with muted olive for the surrounding foliage and trees. All shadows should be cool gray-brown, never pure black. The stone blocks should show individual shapes with subtle color variation.

Position the viewer at a 3/4 elevated perspective, approximately 15-20 degrees above eye level, showing the corner of the building to reveal two facades. Soft, warm morning light enters from the upper left, creating gentle highlights on the limestone and soft shadows.

CRITICAL: The illustration must fade into pure white paper at all edges using a classic vignette technique. The ground plane (lawn area) should dissolve and disappear into white, allowing the building to float on the white background. Background trees should be rendered impressionistically with soft, lost edges that blend into the white. Foliage should be soft and atmospheric while architectural details on the building remain crisp and precise.

The overall feeling should be warm, intimate, and handcrafted - like a traditional architectural rendering you would find in a high-end design magazine. This is NOT a photograph - it should clearly look like a skilled watercolor painting on white paper.
```

## Grok Tips

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| **Be specific** | Grok responds well to detailed descriptions |
| **Mention what NOT to do** | "NOT a photograph" helps prevent photorealistic output |
| **Repeat critical elements** | Emphasize vignette/white background multiple times |
| **Iterate** | Generate 4-6 versions and pick the best match |
| **Use variants** | Try different emphasis versions above |

**Aspect Ratio:** Request 4:3 or landscape orientation for architectural subjects

**If results are too photorealistic:** Add "hand-painted, visible brushstrokes, watercolor paper texture, artistic illustration style"

**If colors are too cool:** Add "warm earth tones, golden undertones, amber highlights, no blue cast"
