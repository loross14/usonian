Synthesized Style Specification

All designs follow the FLW Stained Glass Transom style with these mandatory elements:

  • ViewBox: 320×100 (transom format)
  • Outer frame: 3px stroke #2E2E2E
  • Inner frame: 0.5px stroke, 30% opacity
  • 4 corner squares: 12×12 with +1,+1 shadow
  • Side diamonds: at x=35 and x=285
  • Background: warm cream #FFFEF5
  • LinearGradient fills for all shapes
  • Stroke overlay on all filled shapes
  • White ellipse highlights: -15° rotation
  • Highlight opacity: 0.45
  • Stroke weights: 3px > 1.5px > 1px > 0.5px
  • Class prefixes: d01-, d02-, d03- etc.
