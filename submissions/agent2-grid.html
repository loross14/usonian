The Sheats-Goldstein Residence is a masterwork of organic architecture, featuring Lautner's signature use of concrete, glass, and natural forms. Perched in the hills of Beverly Crest, the home dissolves the boundary between interior and exterior, creating a continuous flow with the landscape. James Goldstein bequeathed the property to LACMA in 2016, ensuring its preservation as a cultural landmark.

Architect Profile

E. Fay Jones

1921 - 2004

AIA Gold Medal 1990

Euine Fay Jones was an American architect from Arkansas, best known for Thorncrown Chapel in Eureka Springs. A student of Frank Lloyd Wright, Jones developed a distinctive approach to organic architecture that harmonized with the Ozark landscape. His work emphasized natural materials, abundant light, and a spiritual connection between structure and site.
