The Compass Bracket Philosophy

Inspired by Virgil Abloh's use of "quotes" to reframe meaning, USONIAN uses the / COMPASS \ as our quotation mark. The architectural drafting tool becomes typography. Every label is framed by the same instrument that draws the homes we celebrate.
