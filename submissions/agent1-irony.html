"THIS IS A BIOGRAPHY" - John Lautner was an American architect known for his innovative and futuristic designs. A former student of Frank Lloyd Wright, Lautner developed his own distinctive style that emphasized organic forms, dramatic structural solutions, and the integration of indoor and outdoor spaces. His work is considered a "masterclass" in California organic modernism.
